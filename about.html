Why We Took LIS 500

We took LIS 500: Code and Power because we want to better understand how technology connects to real-world consequences. As digital tools become more integrated into everyday life, it’s important to consider how power, bias, and inequality can be reflected in data, algorithms, and platform design.

This course helps us think more critically about who benefits from technology, who may be excluded, and what responsibility designers and developers have in building more equitable systems.
