Resource Guide: Implicit Bias & Intersectionality

This page is designed for first-year college students who are learning these terms for the first time.

What is implicit bias?

Implicit bias refers to automatic, unconscious associations that can influence how we perceive and treat others. These biases can show up even when our values say we believe in fairness.

What is the Implicit Bias Test (IBT)?

The IBT is one tool used to explore unconscious patterns in thinking. It can be a starting point for reflection, but it doesn’t define someone’s character.

What is intersectionality?

Intersectionality is the idea that people’s experiences are shaped by multiple identities at once (like race, gender, class, and disability), and these identities can overlap in ways that affect power and opportunity.
