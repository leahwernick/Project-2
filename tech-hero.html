Our Tech Hero

Richa Kaul — CEO of Complyance

Richa Kaul is the CEO and founder of Complyance, a company focused on using artificial intelligence to improve transparency and accountability in corporate compliance and governance. Through her leadership, she works at the intersection of technology, regulation, and ethical responsibility.

Complyance leverages data and AI tools to help organizations understand risk, ensure compliance, and operate more responsibly. In a world where data-driven systems increasingly influence business decisions, Kaul’s work demonstrates how technology can be used not just for efficiency, but for integrity.

Why She Is Our Tech Hero

As women pursuing careers in technology and data, we are inspired by Richa Kaul’s leadership in a space that has historically been male-dominated. Seeing a woman lead an AI-driven company reinforces that innovation and authority in tech are not limited by gender.

What stands out to us most is how she integrates ethics into technological development. In LIS 500, we examine how power shapes digital systems. Kaul’s work reflects that same awareness — recognizing that data and algorithms influence real people and real outcomes.

We admire her commitment to accountability, transparency, and using technology as a force for responsible decision-making. Her career represents the kind of impact we hope to make as future professionals in tech and data analytics.
