Our Tech Hero

Richa Kaul — CEO of Complyance

For our tech hero, we focused on Richa Kaul, who is the CEO and founder of Complyance. Complyance is a company that focuses on using AI to improve accountability in corporate compliance and governance settings. She works at the intersection of technology and ethical responsibility in the workplace.

Complyance helps companies understand risk and ensure greater responsibility in business decision-making. They do this by utilizing data and AI tools, working toward a world where data-driven systems positively influence business decisions. Through her leadership, Richa Kaul demonstrates how technology can be used for accountability and transparency — not just efficiency.

As two women hoping to pursue careers in the field of technology, we look up to Richa Kaul as someone who has excelled in a traditionally male-dominated industry. She shows us that gender does not limit our abilities in technology and inspires us to pursue leadership roles with confidence.

In LIS 500, we examine how technology and society are integrated and how systems shape power and decision-making. Richa Kaul’s work reflects this same idea by showing how technology and algorithms influence real-world outcomes and impact people’s lives. Her commitment to responsible innovation highlights the importance of using technology ethically and demonstrates the impact we can make as future women in technology.
