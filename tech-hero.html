Tech Hero: Joy Buolamwini

Joy Buolamwini is a researcher and activist who focuses on algorithmic bias and fairness in artificial intelligence. Her work highlights how technology can reflect and reinforce inequality, especially when systems are trained on non-diverse data.

Why she matters

Buolamwini has pushed public conversations about accountability in AI. She helps people understand that “neutral” technology can still produce unfair outcomes, and she advocates for building systems that work more equitably for everyone.
